Christopher M. Priebe
Ph.D. Student
University of California San Diego, La Jolla, CA
2022 -

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California San Diego where I am advised by Professor Hadi Esmaeilzadeh. I work on compilers for cross-domain applications, multi-target compilers, and machine-learning-based compiler optimizations.

2018 - 2022

I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Purdue University in 2018. While I was a student, I had the opportunity to work with the Purdue SoCet team and take ECE 43700 “Computer Design and Prototyping” with Professor T. N. Vijaykumar, both of which inspired me to pursue a doctoral degree. I also completed two summer internships at Intel working on pre-silicon verification and an internship at NSWC Crane working on digital design; all three were virtual due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
